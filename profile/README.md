## 🚀 What It Is
Red Giant Magic Bullet for Windows is a suite of color-grading and finishing plugins from Maxon, designed to give video a filmic, professional look. The Magic Bullet suite bundles several plugins together — led by Magic Bullet Looks for cinematic grading, and joined by tools for film emulation, noise reduction, and cosmetic skin work. Everything installs into the editors you already run, so grading and finishing stay part of your normal workflow rather than a separate export-and-reimport chore.

> Magic Bullet Looks is the flagship of the suite: a visual grading environment where you build a look from presets and shape it with easy, on-set-style controls.

## ✨ Key Features
- 🎨 **Magic Bullet Looks** — Build a cinematic grade visually with presets and tune color, exposure, and mood using hands-on controls.
- 📦 **Complete suite** — The Red Giant Magic Bullet suite packs several plugins into one install, from looks to denoise to film styling.
- 🎞️ **Authentic film feel** — Emulate film characteristics and grain so digital footage takes on a warmer, more organic look.
- 🧩 **Works as plugins** — Drop Magic Bullet effects onto clips in Premiere Pro and After Effects for non-destructive grading.
- 💄 **Cosmetic finishing** — Smooth skin and clean up noise to give interviews and portraits a refined, professional finish.

## 🎯 First Steps
1. Quit Premiere Pro or After Effects and run the installer so Red Giant Magic Bullet can register its plugins with each host.
2. Start your editor again and place the shot you want to work on into the timeline.
3. Search for the Magic Bullet Looks effect in your effects panel and apply it to the clip.
4. Open the Looks editor, choose a cinematic preset, and adjust color, contrast, and lighting until the shot feels right.
5. Layer additional suite plugins for film styling or skin smoothing before you render your final output.
